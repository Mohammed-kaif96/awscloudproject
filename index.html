The studio

We design for the Tuesday, not the housewarming.

Most design decisions get made for the day the photographs are taken. We work backwards from an ordinary Tuesday instead — where the light falls at 8am, where the shoes pile up, where a laptop actually gets left open.

That means fewer hero shots and more site visits after the client has lived somewhere for a year. It's slower. It's also why people call us back.

  • Material honestyNothing pretending to be something it isn't.
  • Daylight firstEvery plan starts with the sun, not the floor area.
  • Built to be lived inDurability tested against real habits, not showrooms.

How a project runs

Four stages, no surprises.

  1. 01

    Site & brief

    Two weeks on-site, measuring light and asking questions before a single line is drawn.

  2. 02

    Concept

    One direction, presented in full — plans, models, and material samples in hand.

  3. 03

    Documentation

    Construction drawings detailed enough that contractors stop calling us to ask.

  4. 04

    Build oversight

    Weekly site visits through to handover, and a check-in at the one-year mark.
